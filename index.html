 FAQ'S

Does Bhukamatha was an officially developed plugin?

No, Bhukamatha is developed by SurveyorStories community backed by Village Surveyors 

Can i use Bhukamatha for generating LPMS? Should i need to pay anything for using Bhukamatha?

Yes and also supports Stone map also from version 1.0.1 beta. its up to you weather to use it or not and you need not pay anything for using it

How would Bhukamatha will prevent the miss using of data?

Bhukamatha doesn't hold or provide any data. complete data you processed is lies at your pc. Hence no one can make the LPMs unless they have the data.

Is Team Bhukamatha was interested to Collaborate/ Contribution? 

We are happy to collaborate with you for building better plugin. You can contribute to Bhukamatha in implementing new features, making documentation, creating and updating this portal. you can reach us at surveyorstories@gmail.com

Does Bhukamatha offers any guarantee of work or service 

No we are not give you any guarantee of work or support. We are not responsible for any loss of work, We are only help to fix any bugs identified during the process. its up to you for taking the risk.

What platforms Bhukamatha was supported and is it open source?

Bhukamtha was supported on QGIS an open source software and as like QGIS Bhukamatha was also a opensource plugin.

Is Bhukamatha was open for all?

No Bhukamatha was still under development. so might have bugs which causes issues even though you can use it to test.
